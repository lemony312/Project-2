What kind of places can I get perks at?

We cover a wide range of popular eateries from casual sandwich joints to fine dining restaurants in Manhattan. They are all rated over 3.5 on yelp.

Does everyone see the same list of restaurants/cafes/bars?

No. Depending on your dining frequency, preferences, and feedback from the eateries, we customize the list of eligible perks for you.

I have a favorite eatery that I would like to receive perks from. What can you do?

Please email us the name and the owner/manager of the eatery. We will contact them immediately and try our best to make your wish come true!

Would I always see the same perks?

You will see both our regular and seasonal perks. Share your favorite places with us and we will work to add them to our list.

I am seeing different perks from my friends. Why?

Eateries sometimes have target customer types, and we control who sees the perks in that case. Generally speaking, you can unlock more perks by redeeming more perks, showing up on time for your reservations, and being a nice customer :)

I tried to redeem the perks but didn't work. What is going on?

We are very sorry that this is happening. Sometimes the eateries have new employees who have not been informed about our service. Please ask to speak to a manager in this case and show your screen of DinersCode with the perks. If the problem persists, please email feedback@dinerscode.com. We will respond immediately.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. We are offering this service to you completely free of charge. All you have to do is love eating out!

I am running late to my reservation or I want to cancel a reservation. What do I do?
