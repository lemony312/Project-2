Who We Are

DinersCode is your one-stop destination for the best New York City restaurants perks. You can search for your free offers at restaurants, cafés and bars by neighborhood and price range. The best part: the more you dine, the more perks you get!


Launched in September 2015, DinersCode was inspired by two former executives from JP Morgan and Sony, who were frustrated by the plethora of unreliable sources and ratings on the Internet.


Having been to over 700 restaurants in New York City alone, we curate restaurants in your area through heavy research to ensure the quality and perks you experience. We also work directly with the restaurants to nurture the growth of New York’s dining scene.



Questions?

WHAT KIND OF PLACES CAN I GET PERKS AT?

We cover a wide range of popular eateries from casual sandwich joints to fine dining restaurants in Manhattan. They are all rated over 3.5 on yelp.

DOES EVERYONE SEE THE SAME LIST OF PERKS?

No. Depending on your dining frequency, preferences, and feedback from the eateries, we customize the list of eligible perks for you.

ARE THERE ANY HIDDEN FEES?

No. We are offering this service to you completely free of charge. All you have to do is love eating out!

WOULD I ALWAYS SEE THE SAME PERKS?

You will see both our regular and seasonal perks. Share your favorite places with us and we will work to add them to our list.
